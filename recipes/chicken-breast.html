Chicken Breast Recipe

Description:

This Chicken Breast recipe is taken from this website. "What's the secret to this baked and seasoned chicken breast recipe? It's all in the homemade dry spice rub! The mix of brown sugar, Italian seasoning, and paprika creates the most delectable flavor. This recipe is quick and easy, healthy, and simply one of the best chicken breast recipes for weeknight dinners or meal prep."

"This seasoned chicken breast recipe gets its flavor from the best chicken seasoning, which can be mixed together with a few staples from your pantry and spice cabinet."

Ingredients:

  • 4 large chicken breasts
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 1/2 tablespoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon Italian Seasoning
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1 and 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon pepper

Recipe Steps:

The following recipe makes 4 servings.

  1. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees.
  2. Drizzle the oil over the chicken breasts.
  3. Combine the brown sugar, paprika, Italian seasoning, garlic powder, onion powder, salt, and pepper and rub it all over the chicken breasts until evenly coated.
  4. Place the chicken on a sheet pan lined with parchment paper or in a greased casserole dish.
  5. Bake in the oven for 16-18 minutes or until the internal temperature of the chicken reaches 165 degrees.
