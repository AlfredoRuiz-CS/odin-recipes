Pineapple Barbecue Chicken Pizza Recipe

Description:

This Pineapple Barbecue Chicken Pizza recipe is taken from this website. This easy Pineapple Barbecue Chicken Pizza recipe is prepped in 15 minutes and baked for 12 minutes for a quick pizza meal that's done in under 30 minutes. The classic barbecue chicken pizza is updated to feature fresh pineapple slices that give the pizza a delightful taste.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound homemade or purchased pizza dough
  • 1/2 cup barbecue sauce
  • 1 and 1/2 cups shredded pizza cheese blend
  • 1 and 1/2 cups purchased deli-roasted chicken, chopped
  • 2/3 cup chopped onion
  • 1/2 cup chopped sweet pepper
  • 1/2 cup chopped fresh or canned pineapple, well-drained

Recipe Steps:

The following recipe makes 4 servings.

  1. Press the dough into a 14-inch circle on a baking sheet.
  2. Spread barbecue sauce evenly over the crust and cover with some of the shredded cheese.
  3. Top with chicken, onion, sweet pepper, pineapple and the rest of the cheese.
  4. Bake in a 500Â° oven for 12 to 15 minutes or until cheese is melted and crust is golden brown.
